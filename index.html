About Us

We Innovate Technologies like

Known Aero is an aviation & aerospace company where we aim to build pico-sats, cube-sats and small to heavy-lift launch vehicle. We are also designing and innovating new propulsion technology for rocket and satellites to increase the efficiency and to low the cost of manufacturing. In future we are aiming to build super heavy launcher to transport human from one planet to another. We are planing to use Aerospike nozzle engine which would rapidly bring the launch cost down and in future as we know that the space tourism is going to increase it would be affordable for people to travel from earth to space and beyond. Company is also aiming to build space industry and start astroide mining in future.
